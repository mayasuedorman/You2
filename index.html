Overview

The Scaled Agile Framework (SAFe) is a proven, publicly available framework for implementing agile practices at enterprise scale. It provides a comprehensive approach to aligning teams, improving collaboration, and delivering value to customers faster.

By implementing SAFe, companies can achieve:

  • Improved productivity and time-to-market
  • Enhanced quality and customer satisfaction
  • Increased employee engagement and retention
  • Reduced risk and improved governance
